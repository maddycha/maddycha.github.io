overview

This video was created to bring awareness to the issue of overfishing in response to the UN SDG supporting sustainable fishing.

I decided to focus on a middle-high school audience to bring attention to the issues before bad consumer habits have a chance to develop. I utilized a playful style for the illustrations/motion ending with a call to action to learn more about ways to help.

initial research

"Conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas and marine resources for sustainable development"

In reading into the 2030 agenda for sustainable development by the United Nations, I decided to hone in on Goal 14 in regards to marine life. More specifically, I wanted to speak to 14.4: "By 2020, effectively regulate harvesting and end overfishing, illegal, unreported and unregulated fishing and destructive fishing practices and implement science-based management plans, in order to restore fish stocks in the shortest time feasible, at least to levels that can produce maximum sustainable yield as determined by their biological characteristics".
